⏱️ Motion Study — Work Types

The work a plot goes through, in order — e.g. Soil Transport → Polybag Filling → Polybag Lining → Transplanting → Seedling Maturing → Seedling Open Collection → 3rd Culling. Nothing is fixed: add or remove work types freely, and record the motion study of each — how many days it usually needs per plot, the usual crew size, and notes. These standards drive the Monthly Schedule (planned days prefill) and Life of Plot (on-pace / over-standard flags).

Removing a work type does not delete history — past schedule and plot records keep the work's name. Use ↑ ↓ to keep the list in real working order.
