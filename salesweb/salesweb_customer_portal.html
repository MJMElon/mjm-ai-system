MJM Nursery — Collection Terms & Conditions

1. Collection Responsibility. The customer is solely responsible for arranging adequate transportation to collect the seedlings from MJM Nursery on the booked date and time.

2. Seedling Condition. MJM Nursery guarantees seedlings are in healthy condition at time of collection.

3. Quantity Verification. The customer must verify the quantity received at the time of collection and sign the delivery order (DO).

4. Changes & Cancellations. Booking date changes require a minimum of 48 hours' notice.

5. Access & Safety. The customer's driver and staff must adhere to nursery safety guidelines while on premises.

6. Force Majeure. MJM Nursery reserves the right to reschedule collections in the event of extreme weather or other circumstances beyond our control.
