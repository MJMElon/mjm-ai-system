Meet Carlos — Our Case App

Carlos is MJM's in-house app for logging and tracking problems in the field. Every problem you spot becomes a case — and every case must be resolved within one week.

Spot the Problem

While working in the field, you notice something that isn't right.

Resolve Within 1 Week

Fix it on site and close the case in Carlos within seven days.

The 1-week rule
Once a case is opened in Carlos, it must be resolved and closed within one week. Carlos tracks the open / resolved status of every case.
Carlos

When to Open a Case

Open a Carlos case whenever you spot any of these problems:

Non-satisfied operation work

Any operation work that was not done properly or to standard.

Maintenance of plot

Plots needing upkeep — overgrowth, damage or poor condition.

Maintenance of road

Estate roads needing repair — potholes, erosion or blockages.

Piping leakage

Any leaking pipe, joint or irrigation line.

Other problems spotted

Anything else out of place — if in doubt, open a case.

Carlos

Open a New Case

When you spot a problem, open Carlos and create a New Case with these details.

  • Location — pick the nursery / estate location (e.g. Nursery — UNN 1).
  • Area — choose the specific area within that location.
  • Category — select the type of problem (operation, plot, road, piping, other).
  • Coordinates — keep Automatic on so Carlos records your GPS position.
  • Photo 1 is compulsory — take a clear photo of the problem.

A case can't be submitted without the compulsory photo and coordinates.
